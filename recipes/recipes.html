Protein Bagels

Description

These high protein bagels are super simple to make and taste amazing!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 1/2 cups of greek yogurt
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • Egg wash
  • Everything bagel seasoning or whatever topping you'd like

Instructions

  1. Mix together all ingredients
  2. Knead into a ball
  3. Separate into four balls and shape into bagels
  4. Brush with eggwash and topping
  5. Bake at 350 for 30 minutes
