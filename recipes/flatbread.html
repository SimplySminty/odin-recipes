Flatbread Pizza

Description

Low calorie and high protein flatbread pizza

Ingredients

  • Lavash flatbread
  • Pizza sauce
  • Low fat mozzarella cheese
  • Turkey pepperoni
  • Italian seasoning
  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Spread sauce on pizza and top with cheese
  3. Add pepporoni and italian seasoning
  4. Bake for 15 minutes or until cheese it melted and pepperoni starts to crisp
